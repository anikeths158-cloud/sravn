A typical tour operator focuses on tickets and hotels. We focus on elders’ real needs: health, emotions and dignity.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

A quick overview of common questions families ask before booking a SRAVN Yatra.

Is SRAVN a travel agency or a care service?
SRAVN is a care-first Yatra service. We do handle travel bookings, but our primary focus is elder comfort, safety and companionship during the pilgrimage.
Can you handle elders with BP / diabetes / other conditions?
We can support elders with common lifestyle conditions as long as their doctor has allowed travel. We plan the Yatra with slower pace, adequate rest and food options as per guidance. We do not replace medical professionals.
Do caretakers stay with elders throughout the trip?
Yes. Our caretakers are with them from start to end of the Yatra – at stations, in vehicles, at temples and at the place of stay.
How much time before the Yatra should we contact you?
Ideally 3–4 weeks before travel for smoother planning and better ticket options. For urgent cases, we can check feasibility based on slots.
